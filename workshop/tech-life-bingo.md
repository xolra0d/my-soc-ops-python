# Tech Life Bingo: Developer Edition 🖥️

Break the ice with your fellow developers! Find someone who matches the description in a square to mark it off.

## How to Play
1. Mix and mingle with the group.
2. Ask questions to find matches.
3. When you find a match, ask for a short story or detail!
4. Mark the square.
5. First person to get 5 in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) shouts **"BINGO!"** or **"COMPILED!"**
